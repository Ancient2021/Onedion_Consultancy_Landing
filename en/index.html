Compliance

NIS2 Article 21 —
what does it mean for your business?

Chain liability affects you — even if NIS2 doesn't apply directly

From 2025, the NIS2 directive also applies to SMEs that supply larger organizations. Under Article 21, large companies are required to verify the cybersecurity of their suppliers. If you supply to such organizations, they can demand that you demonstrably work securely — even if you're not directly subject to NIS2.
